Notes & Reflections

About the Game

Cash Catcher is a satirical game that visualizes political donations during the 2024 U.S. presidential campaign. By collecting falling “donations” from billionaire donors, players confront how disproportionate wealth influences political momentum.

The player’s win is intentionally ironic — collecting millions in seconds reflects a reality that’s both outrageous and normalized.

Why Elon Musk?

Elon Musk was chosen as a symbolic donor due to his high-profile political commentary, corporate influence, and wealth. This character represents the blurred line between private interests and public leadership in today’s politics.

Fact Spotlight

By the end of Trump's presidential campaign, Elon Musk had spent $277 million to elect Trump and allied Republicans — making him the largest individual political donor of the 2024 election and the largest since at least 2010 (outside of self-funded candidates). His donations primarily went to his super PAC, America PAC.
